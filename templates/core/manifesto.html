Five Pillars of Development

Our manifesto is built on five fundamental pillars that address the core needs of our communities

Livelihood Security

Ensuring sustainable and dignified livelihoods for fishing communities and allied sectors through modern infrastructure, fair pricing, and protection of traditional rights.

  • Modern Fishing Infrastructure: Develop state-of-the-art fishing harbors, cold storage facilities, and processing units in every coastal district
  • Fair Price Guarantee: Establish minimum support price (MSP) for all major fish varieties to ensure fair returns to fishermen
  • Traditional Rights Protection: Legally protect traditional fishing grounds and water bodies from corporate exploitation
  • Subsidized Equipment: Provide fishing equipment, boats, and nets at subsidized rates with easy loan facilities
  • Market Access: Create direct market linkages and eliminate middlemen to ensure better prices for fishermen

Education & Skill Development

Building a brighter future through quality education, vocational training, and skill development programs tailored to community needs.

  • Free Education: Provide free quality education from primary to higher secondary level for children from fishing communities
  • Scholarship Programs: Offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for college and professional courses
  • Skill Training Centers: Establish modern skill development centers in fishing villages offering courses in various trades
  • Digital Literacy: Launch comprehensive digital literacy programs to bridge the technology gap
  • Marine Studies Institutes: Create specialized institutes for marine science, fisheries management, and aquaculture

Healthcare & Social Welfare

Comprehensive healthcare and social security measures to ensure the wellbeing and dignity of every community member.

  • Free Healthcare: Provide free medical treatment at government hospitals for all community members
  • Mobile Medical Units: Deploy mobile healthcare units to reach remote fishing villages
  • Insurance Schemes: Implement comprehensive health and life insurance schemes at nominal premiums
  • Pension Plans: Ensure pension benefits for elderly fishermen and their widows
  • Disaster Relief: Create special relief funds for natural disasters and fishing season losses

Political Representation

Ensuring adequate representation and voice for fishing communities in governance and decision-making processes at all levels.

  • Reserved Seats: Advocate for reserved seats in legislative bodies proportionate to community population
  • Fisheries Board: Establish autonomous Fisheries Development Boards with community representation
  • Policy Participation: Ensure community participation in formulating policies affecting fishing and allied sectors
  • Local Governance: Strengthen Panchayati Raj institutions with adequate powers for community development
  • Leadership Development: Create programs to develop political leadership from within the community

Infrastructure & Technology

Modern infrastructure and technology adoption to improve living standards and boost economic opportunities in fishing communities.

  • Roads & Connectivity: Construct all-weather roads connecting fishing villages to markets and towns
  • Electricity & Water: Ensure 24x7 electricity supply and safe drinking water in all fishing hamlets
  • Internet Access: Provide high-speed internet connectivity to enable digital empowerment
  • Weather Forecasting: Deploy advanced weather forecasting systems and warning mechanisms for fishermen safety
  • Modern Housing: Implement housing schemes with proper sanitation and living facilities
