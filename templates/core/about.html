Our Story

Born from the struggles of the fishing and sailing communities, Nishad Party stands as a beacon of hope for the marginalized sections of society.

Our Beginning

Nishad Party was founded with a vision to represent the unheard voices of fishermen, sailors, and other marginalized communities across India. Our party emerged from the grassroots, understanding the real challenges faced by these communities.

The name "Nirbal Indian Shoshit Hamara Aam Dal" reflects our commitment to standing with the weak, the oppressed, and the common people of our nation.

Our Community

We represent millions of fishermen, boat operators, sailors, and allied workers who form the backbone of India's maritime and fishing industry. Their livelihoods depend on the rivers, seas, and water bodies that sustain our nation.

Beyond the fishing community, we stand with all marginalized sections including farmers, laborers, and small business owners who need a strong voice in politics.

Our Commitment

Our commitment goes beyond mere political representation. We work tirelessly to ensure that government policies are inclusive and beneficial to the communities we serve. From securing fishing rights to ensuring fair compensation for environmental damages, we fight for justice at every level.

We believe in sustainable development that protects both livelihoods and the environment. Our approach combines traditional wisdom with modern solutions to create a better future for our communities.

Our Core Values
