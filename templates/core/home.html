About Nishad Party

Nishad Party (Nirbal Indian Shoshit Hamara Aam Dal) is a political party dedicated to the rights, welfare, and empowerment of the fishermen and sailor community across India.

Community-Centered Policies - Policies designed by and for the fishing community
Transparent Governance - Open and accountable leadership
Economic Empowerment - Programs to improve livelihoods
Social Justice - Fighting for equal rights and opportunities
